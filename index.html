How To Play?

Blix is a fast-paced and attention-based game where the player must score as many points as possible within a short amount of time by tapping on images that fall from the top of the screen.

The timer starts immediately when the game begins. Players must quickly react and tap on falling images to earn points before the time runs out.

Bombs occasionally fall alongside other images. If the player taps on a bomb, they will lose 10 points, and the screen will flash red as a warning. This adds an extra layer of challenge.

The game ends automatically when the countdown reaches zero. The final score depends on how many points the player has collected during that time.

As the player earns points, those can be used in the in-game shop to purchase upgrades, such as extra time. This allows for higher scores and longer gameplay sessions.
